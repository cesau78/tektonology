# Assembly Guide
1. **Clean Surfaces:** Remove any stringing from the internal wedge cavity.
2. **Apply Glue:** Place 2-3 drops of Loctite 406 inside the PLA upper boot cavity.
3. **Press-Fit:** Press the TPU floor pad wedge into the PLA cavity until flush.
4. **Cure:** Let sit for 60 seconds before installing on the pew.
